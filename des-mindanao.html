MINDANAO

Mindanao, the southernmost major island of the Philippines, is a captivating tapestry of diverse landscapes, rich cultural traditions, and breathtaking natural wonders. From the majestic Mount Apo, the highest peak in the country, to the pristine beaches of Siargao and Samal, Mindanao offers a unique blend of adventure, cultural immersion, and natural beauty. Explore the vibrant cities of Davao and Zamboanga, delve into the fascinating history and traditions of the indigenous tribes, or simply relax on the island's pristine shores. Mindanao is a destination that promises an unforgettable journey filled with discovery and wonder.

Places to Explore

Aliwagwag Falls
Cateel, Davao Oriental

considered to be one of the longest waterfalls in the Philippines. It is good to visit on months when it's rainy because that's when the waters would be raging and the whirring echoes they make, makes for a more exciting experience.

Cloud 9
General Luna, Surigao del Norte

is a world-renowned surfing destination known for its challenging waves and laid-back vibe. This iconic surfing spot boasts a natural reef break that produces powerful, hollow waves, attracting surfers from all over the globe. The area also offers a vibrant beach culture with its lively bars, restaurants, and accommodations, making it a perfect blend of adventure and relaxation.

Dahilayan Adventure Park
Brgy. Dahilayan, Manolo Fortich, Bukidnon

is an outdoor enthusiast's paradise, offering a thrilling array of activities amidst stunning mountain scenery. From exhilarating zipline rides soaring through the forest canopy to the adrenaline-pumping "superman" zipline, Dahilayan provides a unique blend of adventure and breathtaking views. Other attractions include a challenging obstacle course, a giant swing, and a scenic chairlift ride, making it a perfect destination for thrill-seekers.
